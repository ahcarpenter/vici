## Decisions Made
- Used `replicas: 0` in both Jaeger YAML configs — single-node OpenSearch cannot allocate replicas (yellow cluster health otherwise)
- Used `ALWAYS_ON` over `ParentBasedTraceIdRatio(1.0)` — simpler, unambiguous, no parent-based override behavior
- `deployment.environment` derives from `settings.inngest_dev` bool (True -> "development", False -> "production")
